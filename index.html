About OneLeaf

My fascination with both music and games has led me on an exciting creative odyssey. As a seasoned artist in the world of sound, I've had the privilege of giving voice to virtual worlds, adding layers of emotion, tension, and ambience that players can feel as much as they hear. I can make almost all styles of music and sounds, for a lot of different genres ! Collaboration lies at the heart of my process. I thrive on building strong partnerships with developers, understanding their vision, and working hand in hand to ensure that every sound resonates authentically with their creation. Together, we sculpt worlds that players can not only see and play, but truly inhabit.

As you navigate through my portfolio, I invite you to listen with intention and immerse yourself in the stories that sound can tell. Join me on this journey where melodies become memories and where the symphony of gameplay comes alive.

Thank you for visiting. Let's embark on a harmonious adventure in crafting unforgettable auditory realms together. Let's embark on a harmonious adventure together.
